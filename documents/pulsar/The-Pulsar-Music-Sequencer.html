The Pulsar Music Sequencer

The Pulsar Music Sequencer is a music sequencer program which allows users to write music as Lisp Scheme programs.

In this system, musical notes and other informations are written as Scheme’s association lists.

The musical notes can be dynamically generated on-the-fly; users can interact with the programs to affect where their music go.

With this system, you can learn music and Lisp Scheme at same time.

Pulsar Sequencer is written in Java8 and Kawa, and processes MIDI data via with Jack Audio Connection Kit.

This manual is for Pulsar Sequencer version 0.1.
